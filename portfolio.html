G TRAIN COVER- THIRDSTORY

THIS WAS A COVER I RECORDED FOR AN AUDIO PROJECT. IT'S CALLED G TRAIN BY THIRDSTORY. I RECORDED AND PRODUCED THIS MYSELF WITH THE HELP OF JUSTIN DALISAY, WHO PLAYED THE GUITAR FOR ME.

IF I BELIEVE YOU COVER- THE 1975

THE 1975 IS MY FAVORITE BAND, SO I DECIDED TO DO A COVER OF ONE OF THEIR SONGS CALLED IF I BELIEVE YOU. I PERFORMED WITH JUSTIN DALISAY, WHO PLAYED THE GUITAR AS WELL AS RECORDED AND PRODUCED THIS COVER.

WITHOUT YOU (ORIGINAL)

TROYE SIVAN IS ONE OF MY FAVORITE ARTISTS AND HIS ALBUM, BLUE NEIGHBOURHOOD, LEFT SUCH AN IMPACT ON ME. I COULD REALLY RELATE TO EVERY ONE OF HIS SONGS, SO I DECIDED TO DO A LITTLE MEDLEY OF SOME OF THEM.

LET ME (ORIGINAL)

I'VE WRITTEN MANY SONGS, BUT THIS IS PROBABLY MY FAVORITE. I WRITE SONGS THAT I KNOW PEOPLE CAN RELATE TO, SO THIS SONG WILL ALWAYS HAVE A SPECIAL PLACE IN MY HEART.

THIS SONG IS VERY IMPORTANT TO ME FOR MANY REASONS. IT WAS THE FIRST SONG THAT I WROTE AND RECORDED AND IT WAS ALSO ABOUT A VERY TRUE EXPERIENCE THAT I WENT THROUGH AND WAS ABLE TO OVERCOME.

SOMEBODY ELSE COVER- THE 1975

THIS WAS JUST A COVER I DID OF SOMEBODY ELSE BY THE 1975. I WASN'T TOO PROUD OF THIS COVER, SO I REALLY WANT TO DO IT OVER AGAIN. THIS IS MY FAVORITE SONG AND I WANT TO DO IT JUSTICE.
